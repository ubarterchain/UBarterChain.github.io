    •   Market Page

    There are four subpages in Market page: Send, Barter; List; Post

    1. Send subpage: send your token to someone else.
      • Choose (1) or input (2) symbol of the token tobe sent.
      • Input (3) the TXID with tobe sent token; choose (4) the related vout index of the TX; click "+/-Sender" will add a sender. Repeat this step can add upto 10 sender.
      • Input (5) the receiver's check or click "Check" button to input your check; input (6) the amount for this receiver; click "+/-Receiver" will add a receiver. Repeat this step can add upto 10 receiver.
      • Inout your account password; click "Open" button to open your account.
      • Click "Create TX", "Confirm TX", and "Send TX" buttons.
      • If the sending is successful, the TXID will showup (8).
    2. Barter subpage: setup your account
      • Blocking nodes setting
      • Connection nodes setting
      • Stop a connection node - input node's address; click "Stop Connection" button.
      • Open blocking nodes list - click "Blocking List" button.
      • Clean blocking nodes list - click "Clean Blocking" button.
      • Open connection nodes list - click "Connection List" button.
    3. List subpage: setup your local node
      • Blocking nodes setting
      • Connection nodes setting
      • Stop a connection node - input node's address; click "Stop Connection" button.
      • Open blocking nodes list - click "Blocking List" button.
      • Clean blocking nodes list - click "Clean Blocking" button.
      • Open connection nodes list - click "Connection List" button.
    4. Post subpage: setup your local node
      • Blocking nodes setting
      • Connection nodes setting
      • Stop a connection node - input node's address; click "Stop Connection" button.
      • Open blocking nodes list - click "Blocking List" button.
      • Clean blocking nodes list - click "Clean Blocking" button.
      • Open connection nodes list - click "Connection List" button.
