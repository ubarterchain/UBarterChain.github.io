    •   Market Page

    There are four subpages in Market page: Send, Barter; List; Post

    1. Send subpage: send your token to someone else.
      • Choose (1) or input (2) the symbol of the token tobe sent.
      • Input (3) the TXID with tobe sent token; choose (4) the related vout index of the TX; click "+/-Sender" will add a sender. Repeat this step can add upto 10 sender.
      • Input (5) the receiver's check or click "Check" button to input your check; input (6) the amount for this receiver; click "+/-Receiver" button will add a receiver. Repeat this step can add upto 10 receiver.
      • Inout (7) your account password; click "Open" button to open your account.
      • Click "Create TX", "Confirm TX", and "Send TX" buttons.
      • If the sending TX is passed, the TXID will showup (8).
    2. Barter subpage: barter 1 kind of token with another kind of token
      • Choose (1) or input (2) the symbol of the token tobe barter out.
      • Choose (3) the time limit for this barter transaction.
      • Input (4) the TXID with tobe barter out token; choose (5) the related vout index of the TX; click "+/-Sender" will add the sender.
      • Choose (6) or input (7) the symbol of the token tobe barter in.
      • Input (8) tobe received check; Input (9) tobe received amount; click "+/-Receiver" button will add the receiver.
      • Inout (10) your account password; click "Open" button to open your account.
      • Click "Create TX", "Confirm TX", and "Send TX" buttons.
      • If the bartering TX is passed, the TXID will showup (11).
    3. List subpage: list your new token for bartering
      • Input (1) the symbol of your new token tobe listed.
      • Input (2) the name of your new token.
      • Choose (3) to set the smallest unit for your new token.
      • Input (4) the total tobe listed amount of your new token.
      • Input (5, 6, 7) or click the "Check" buttons to add thress checks.
      • Input (8) the rate of cashout for your new token.
      • Input (9) to post the most important info about your new token.
      • Input (10) the check to save new token; Input (11) amount tobe saved; click ""+/-Receiver" button will add a receiver. Repeat this step can add upto 10 receiver.
      • Inout (12) your account password; click "Open" button to open your account.
      • Click "Create TX", "Confirm TX", and "Send TX" buttons.
      • If the listing TX is passed, the TXID will showup (13).
    4. Cashout subpage: cashout your token
      • Choose (1) or input (2) the symbol of the token tobe cashout.
      • Choose (3) the way to cashout.
      • Input (4) the TXID with tobe cashout token; choose (5) the related vout index of the TX; click "+/-Sender" will add a cashouted. Repeat this step can add upto 10 cashouted.
      • Input (6) the detail cashout info.
      • Input (7) the verify check for the token (can find from "Browser" -> "Product List" -> "cashout check".
      • Inout (9) your account password; click "Open" button to open your account.
      • Click "Create TX", "Confirm TX", and "Send TX" buttons.
      • If the cashout TX is passed, the TXID will showup (8).
